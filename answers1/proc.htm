Emergency Flashlight

Adjustment/Test
33-53-01

Adjustment

  1. Remove cover from flashlight holder.
  2. Remove flashlight from holder.
  3. Replace flashlight in holder.
  4. Replace cover on holder.

Operational Test

  1. Remove cover from flashlight holder.
  2. Remove flashlight from holder.
  3. Turn flashlight on.
  4. Turn flashlight off.
  5. Replace flashlight in holder.
  6. Replace cover on holder.
